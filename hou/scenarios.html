Window Systems in Houston, TX

The unshaded insulating glass units (IGUs) where specific for a hot climate, including window systems (scenarios) that meet the ENERGY STAR® requirements. Scenarios included unshaded windows, windows with Venetian blinds-between-glass (BBGs), 1-inch interior Venetian blinds, and with 2 types of interior cellular blinds. Blind slats have a positive angle when tipped up and a negative angle when tipped down roomside. See the modeling and simulation methodology for more information.
