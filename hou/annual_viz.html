Annual Energy in Houston, TX

The annual energy in kBtus and costs in dollars are displayed per window system.

The annual difference in cost for each window system compared to a base case is also displayed. The data is sorted by choosing a base case (dual clear: unshaded or with a Venetian blind at 0-degrees).

Blind slats have a positive angle when tipped up and a negative angle when tipped down roomside. See the modeling methodology for more information.
