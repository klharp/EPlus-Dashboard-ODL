Data & Visualizations of EnergyPlus Simulations

Daily & Hourly Performance in Minneapolis, MN

The data is filtered by choosing a base case (dual clear: unshaded or with a Venetian blind at 0-degrees) and a percent reduction in cost from on the selected base case.

After specifying conditions for the base case, percent cost savings, month(s), and day(s), The values in the table meet the specified conditions and displays the hourly cost and the illumination levels that are greater than 200 lux. If the table is displaying no data, then there are no scenarios that meet the specified conditions.

Blind slats have a positive angle when tipped up and a negative angle when tipped down roomside. See the modeling and simulation methodology for more information.
